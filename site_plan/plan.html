Site Plan: Me and Her

Site Name

Me and Her

The name represents a personal space to share our love story and allow other users to share theirs. It reflects intimacy, connection, and community.

Possible domain: meandherstories.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to be a place where experiences and love stories can be shared, with sections dedicated to our story, photos of important moments, and a space for others to submit their own stories.

Scenarios

  • “How can I submit my own story to appear on the site?”
  • “Where can I see the wedding photos and memories?”
