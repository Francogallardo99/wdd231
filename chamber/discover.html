Negotiation of the "Grains and Future" Agreement

At the Córdoba Chamber of Commerce, directors are negotiating a crucial agreement with agricultural producers to improve provincial roads. Tensions are rising, as business owners claim massive losses due to the poor state of the roads, while producers insist on the need for this investment for regional competitiveness.

Launch of the "Young Cordoban Entrepreneur" Program

The main hall is full of university students and young professionals, eager for the launch of a program that offers mentorship and seed capital to new businesses. The Chamber's president inspires attendees with speeches about innovation, while the young people, full of energy and optimism, dream of their projects' success.

Emergency Meeting on the "Water Crisis"

Owners of small and medium-sized businesses are holding an emergency meeting due to water cuts that threaten to paralyze their operations. The affected parties, some angry and others desperate, are seeking an urgent solution to avoid economic losses and are discussing whether to lobby the government or negotiate a fairer rationing system.
